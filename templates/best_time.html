Best Time to Visit Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a beautiful tropical destination that can be visited year-round. However, the best time to visit depends on which part of the island you plan to explore.

West and South Coast

The ideal time to visit the stunning beaches of Galle, Mirissa, and Hikkaduwa is from December to March.

Central Highlands

If you are planning to visit Ella, Nuwara Eliya, or Kandy, the best weather is generally from January to April.
